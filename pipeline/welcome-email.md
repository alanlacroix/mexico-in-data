# Beehiiv welcome email

Use the single Beehiiv welcome email, not a welcome automation as well. Running both sends two welcome messages.

The site already asks Beehiiv to send the welcome email after a subscriber confirms. The email still has to be configured and enabled in Beehiiv under `Settings > Emails > Preset Emails > Welcome Email`.

## Subject

You're subscribed to The Mexico Brief

## Preview text

One email every Monday. Read the latest edition now.

## Body

Thanks for signing up.

The Mexico Brief arrives every Monday morning. If you read nothing else about Mexico that week, you should still know what mattered, especially in the economy, politics, and the relationship with the US.

Every number and claim links to the original source, so you can go deeper when you want. I'll tell you what I think too, but I'll give you the information behind it so you can come to your own conclusion.

**[Read the latest edition →](https://mexicobrief.com/latest-edition)**

That's it. One email a week. You can reply to any issue.

## Beehiiv settings

- Sender name: `Alan Lacroix · The Mexico Brief`
- Reply-to: an inbox Alan checks
- Welcome email: enabled
- Double opt-in: enabled
- Smart Nudge: enabled if desired
- Opt-in redirect: `https://mexicobrief.com/latest-edition`
- Do not also enable a Signed Up welcome automation

Test the whole sequence with a new address: subscribe, receive confirmation, confirm, land on the latest-edition page, receive the welcome email, click the latest-edition link, and reply.
