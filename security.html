Security

The homicide rate fell to ~26 per 100,000 in 2024

Mexico recorded about 26 homicides per 100,000 people in 2024, down from a peak of about 29 in 2018. The rate is the most comparable official measure of lethal violence across states.

The numbers now

About 90% of crimes never get reported to police (ENVIPE, INEGI), so reported-crime counts track filing habits, not danger. The board shows one metric, the homicide rate.
