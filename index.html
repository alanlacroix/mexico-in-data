New to Mexico: the basics

Mexico has 131 million people and a ~$1.9 trillion economy, the world's ~14th-largest.1 It is the largest US trading partner. Mexico mostly sells manufactured goods abroad, and about 80% of its exports go to the United States.2 It elects a president every six years. Claudia Sheinbaum of Morena holds the office through 2030.3
